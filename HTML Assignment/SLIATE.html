OTHER SLIATE INSTITUIONS IN SRI LANKA



FACILITIES
CANTEENS

The cafeteria offer meals and beverages (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner and refreshments) to students and employees at affordable rates with a broad selection of verities. The canteens are available in all ATIs


STUDENT HOSTELS
Hostel facilities are supplied for a limited number of students by the some ATIs. These hostels are situated in the ATI premises and outside.


STUDENT COUNSELING

Members of the academic staff have been appointed to advise the students on day to day problems, matters relating to courses and examinations. The students can meet the counselors during the allocated hours for the counseling. The details of counselors will be displayed by the Institutes.


SPORTS AND GAMES

Sport facilities are available in each ATI’s. Indoor and Outdoor sport facilities are available for students. These sporting facilities include Cricket, Volleyball, Netball, carom, chess and athletics. The playground is situated in a very beautiful atmosphere. Students can use these facilities to improve their mental wellbeing and physical which will help them to maintain a healthy life style. There is an annual Inter ATI Sports day in which students can prove their talents.


OTHER FACILITIES

  • Language cum IT laboratories with multimedia facilities and workshop facilities etc.
  • Library and Internet facilities
  • In-plant Training and Industrial visits
